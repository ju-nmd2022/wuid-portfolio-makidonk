This is my take on the stylization-assignment in my course "Fundamentals of Graphic Design". To praise my Swedishness, I made a simplification of Pippi Longstocking (Långstrump in Swedish) where I wanted to fit both her and her animals into the image.

At first, I made a version with only her braid sticking out from the side of the image, and that was honestly enough for people to recognize her, but I wanted to try out this idea where the horse would be the background and then Pippi and her monkey would be kind of carve-outs and ended up loving the results! It may be a little hard to see it at first, but once you see it, you can't unsee it.

I started off with sketching in an app called Procreate on my Ipad. Then I played around with the layout for a looong time, but it was so fun! When I was satisfyed with the layout I then made a final clean version in Procreate and then sent it over to Illustrator and traced it so that it became vectorized.
