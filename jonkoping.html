What to do

Here comes a list of things that i have loved doing in Jönköping while growing up here:

  • Escape House

    Get locked up in a room with your friends and try to solve problems to get out in time!
    Location :
    Price : 275 sek/pers (225 kr/student)

  • Prison Island

    Experience a prison-adventure where you solve 25+ cells and collect points together in a group
    Location :
    Price : 220 sek/pers for 1,5 h (1-3h)

  • Shop on Asecs

    Asecs is a big mall with every kind of store you can think of. It also contains many different restaurants and cosy cafes, and even a skate park!
    Location :

Where to go out

Because I turnes 18 during the covid-times i haven't really been going out that much, but i still have a few favorite going-out-places to share:

  • Bongo

    Bongo is a cosy, "pb" vibe pub (Pb - refering to the school "Per Brahegymnasiet" in Jönköping which is known for having artsy and creatively expressive students). Visit this bar if you want a nice place to sit and talk with your friends.
    Location :

  • Amor

    Amor is the place to go for shots. They offer many different flavours, and different flavours every time you visit. 5 shots for 100 sek. (dulce de leche is my fav)
    Location :

Where to eat

I gave some examples of my favorite foods in Jönköping on the main-page, but Jönköping has more good food to offer! Here are some more of my recommendations:

  • McDonalds (by asecs)

    Yes, this is just an ordinary McDonalds but its' drive through is open 24/7, so this is da place for a good midnight snack with your friends in the car. Or, you know, by yourself.
    Location :

  • Sushi Bar

    Sushi bar does ordinary sushis, they taste very good but nothing special. The reason tho for them to be qualified for this list, is that they make very big bites, so you get much for the money.
    Location :
